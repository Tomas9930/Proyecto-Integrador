About

We are a web art gallery created with the purpose of disseminating works of art by prominent authors. It is open to receiving your artistic proposal.

We publish well-known works by established artists as well as receive different types of works in painting, sculpture, drawings, etc. from young artists who need our support.

This page is willing to receive all types of constructive criticism that tend to improve our work and provide a service to both specialized art critics and different artists or students who are taking their first steps.

We hope that you visit this page so that you can enjoy the works on display. We welcome your suggestions to show other works that can inspire the world in the future.

We remind you that a contest for young artists on the theme "regional landscapes" will soon be held. The rules are detailed on our social networks and the winner will exhibit their works for a week on this page. Registration is open until September 14 of this year.
